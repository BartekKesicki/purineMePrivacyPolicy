2 Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data the App collects, where it is stored, how it may be shared with third-party service providers, and what rights you have regarding your data.

The App is available in English and Polish. This document is the authoritative English version. A Polish translation may be provided separately for convenience; in the event of conflict, the English version prevails.

Key point: Purine Me has no user accounts, no registration, and no cloud sync of your personal health data. Almost all data is stored exclusively on your device.

3 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All of the following data is stored in a local SQLite database or in device SharedPreferences (Android) / UserDefaults (iOS) and never transmitted to our servers (we operate no backend of our own).

3.1 User Profile

  • Display name (optional)
  • Age, weight, height
  • Gender (male / female / transgender)
  • Preferred measurement units (metric / US)

3.2 Nutritional & Health Records

  • Meal logs: name, date/time, ingredients, portion weight (g), calories (kcal), estimated uric acid / purine values
  • Food product catalogue (built-in + user-created): name, category, kcal, estimated uric acid content
  • Hydration logs: volume (ml) with timestamp
  • Weight history: value with timestamp
  • Dietary plans (Premium): plan parameters, daily meals, ingredient lists, purine budget, uric acid model metadata
  • Demo plan (Free tier): stored as JSON in SharedPreferences

3.3 App Preferences & State

  • UI section visibility (calories, BMI, prompts)
  • Medical disclaimer acceptance status
  • Premium / trial subscription status (local cache; authoritative status held by RevenueCat / app store)
  • Dietary plan shopping list (item-purchased flags)
  • Notification settings (enabled types, quiet hours, schedules)
  • Smart-push engagement metrics: last app-open timestamp, meal-log streak, demo-plan display counter, notification history, cooldown timers
  • Localized notification text templates (used by WorkManager / BackgroundFetch)

3.4 Technical / Operational Data

  • Last meal timestamp, meal count, Premium prompt counter
Data we do not collect: e-mail address, phone number, postal address, GPS location, camera / microphone data, contacts, social-account credentials, or any data via our own servers.

5 Health Data & Medical Disclaimer

The App processes data that may constitute health-related personal data under GDPR Article 9, including body weight, height, estimated uric acid levels, caloric intake, hydration, and BMI. This data is stored exclusively on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

By accepting the in-app medical disclaimer during onboarding (and again before generating a dietary plan), you explicitly consent to the local processing of this health-related data for the sole purpose of operating the App's educational and lifestyle features. Your consent is the legal basis under GDPR Art. 6(1)(a) and Art. 9(2)(a).

Medical Disclaimer: All calculations and estimates provided by Purine Me — including estimated uric acid levels, BMI, BMR, and caloric values — are based on mathematical formulas and publicly available nutritional data. They are provided for informational and educational purposes only and do not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making changes to your diet or medical regimen.

6 Push Notifications

The App may request permission to send you local (on-device) notifications. You may grant or deny this permission at the system level. If permission is denied, all notification options are disabled in the App's UI.

The following notification types are available (each individually toggle-able in Settings):

  • Daily meal-log reminders
  • Diet variety suggestions (Demo plan)
  • Gentle return reminders
  • Weekly meal-logging streak updates
  • Weekend meal ideas

A global 24-hour cooldown is enforced — at most one notification per day is sent. Quiet Hours (default 22:00–08:00, configurable) suppress all notifications during the defined window.

The App tracks lightweight engagement signals (last-open timestamp, log streak, display counters) in SharedPreferences to decide when a notification is relevant. This data never leaves your device.

You may revoke notification permission at any time in your device's system settings (Android: Settings → Apps → Purine Me → Notifications; iOS: Settings → Notifications → Purine Me).

7 Premium Subscription

Purine Me offers an optional Premium subscription that unlocks additional features (full dietary plan generator, shopping list, meal-plan logging, trial period).

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Google Play (Android) or the Apple App Store (iOS). We receive only a subscription-status entitlement and an anonymous RevenueCat customer ID — never your payment details.

To manage or cancel your subscription, use the respective store's subscription management page:

  • Android: Google Play Subscriptions
  • iOS: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions

Some Premium screens may enable a screenshot-protection flag to prevent accidental capture of your dietary plan content. This operates entirely on-device via the system's FLAG_SECURE (Android) or equivalent (iOS) mechanism.

8 Advertising & Consent (AdMob / UMP)

Free-tier users see ads served by Google AdMob. Ads are displayed as banners (dashboard and product screens) and interstitials (e.g., after adding a meal, within the demo plan).

EEA, UK & Switzerland users — UMP Consent

In accordance with EU/EEA data protection law and Google's EU User Consent Policy, users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and Switzerland are shown a consent form (UMP — User Messaging Platform) before personalised ads are displayed.

  • If you consent to personalised advertising: AdMob may use your advertising identifier and behavioural signals to show relevant ads.
  • If you decline or withdraw consent: only non-personalised ads are shown. You will not receive interest-based advertising.

You can review or change your ad-consent choice at any time via Settings → Privacy & Data in the App, or by revoking the advertising identifier in your device settings (Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking).

Premium subscribers do not see AdMob advertisements.

9 Data Storage, Security & Retention

Storage

Personal data is stored in a local SQLite database (purine_meORM.db) and in device-level key-value stores (SharedPreferences / UserDefaults). We do not operate cloud storage for your personal or health data.

Security

Access to your device's data is controlled by the operating system's app sandboxing. We rely on Android and iOS security models to protect your data from other apps. We do not guarantee encryption of the local database at rest; security is inherent to the device's own storage protections.

OS Backups

Your device's operating system (Android/Google Backup, iCloud) may automatically back up app data, including the App's database and preferences. This backup behaviour is controlled by your OS settings, not by us.

Retention & Deletion

Your data persists on your device until you delete it. The App provides a built-in Clear Data function (Settings → Privacy & Data → Clear All Data) that permanently deletes the entire local database and all preferences, then restarts the onboarding flow. This action is irreversible.

You may also uninstall the App at any time; on most devices, uninstallation removes all locally stored app data (subject to OS backup settings).

Data held by third-party services (Crashlytics, RevenueCat) is subject to their own retention policies. Contact those providers directly to exercise deletion rights regarding data they hold.

10 International Data Transfers

Your health and profile data does not leave your device and therefore is not subject to international transfer by us.

However, the third-party SDKs we use (Firebase Crashlytics, Google AdMob, RevenueCat) are operated by companies headquartered in the United States. When these services process data (crash reports, ad signals, subscription status), that data may be transferred to and processed in the USA or other countries outside the European Economic Area.

Google and RevenueCat rely on the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and/or the EU–U.S. Data Privacy Framework as a legal basis for international data transfers. Details can be found in their respective privacy policies linked in Section 4.

11 Your Rights (GDPR & Applicable Law)

Where GDPR or equivalent law applies, you have the following rights regarding your personal data. Because most data is stored locally, many rights are exercised directly on your device.

Right How to exercise it
Right of access — know what data is held All personal/health data is on your device. You can view it directly in the App.
Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") Use Settings → Privacy & Data → Clear All Data to permanently delete all local data. For third-party data, contact the relevant provider.
Right to rectification Edit your profile and meal logs directly within the App.
Right to data portability The App does not currently offer a data-export function. Your data is stored in a standard SQLite file; technically proficient users may access it via device file management tools.
Right to withdraw consent Withdraw ad-personalisation consent in Settings → Privacy & Data. Withdraw notification permission in device system settings. Withdrawal does not affect lawfulness of prior processing.
Right to object / restrict processing Contact us at the address in Section 14. Note: because we hold no cloud copy of your health data, practical processing restrictions are achieved by using the Clear Data feature.
Right to lodge a complaint You may lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority (e.g., UODO in Poland; ICO in the UK; your local Data Protection Authority in the EU).

Legal Bases for Processing

  • GDPR Art. 6(1)(a) — Consent: local processing of health-related data (accepted via in-app disclaimer); personalised advertising (UMP consent); push notifications (OS permission).
  • GDPR Art. 6(1)(b) — Contract performance: operating core App features (meal logging, dietary plan generation) as the service you installed the App to use.
  • GDPR Art. 9(2)(a) — Explicit consent for health data: processing body-weight, estimated uric acid, and similar health-related information for the App's educational features.

12 Children's Privacy

Purine Me is intended for users aged 16 and above. The App is not directed at children under 16 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 16 has entered data into the App, please use the Clear Data function (Section 9) to remove all locally stored data, and contact us (Section 14) regarding any data held by third-party services.
